Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a virtual tour cost? +

Our tours start at $999 for standard spaces under 2,000 square feet. For larger hospitality venues requiring interactive hotspots, custom menus, and lead generation forms, pricing typically ranges from $1,999 to $4,500 based on square footage and technical requirements.

How long does the shoot take? +

Most venue shoots take between 2 to 4 hours on-site. We specialize in hospitality and understand the need to shoot around operating hours, minimizing disruption to your staff and guests.

Where is the tour hosted? +

Your tour is hosted on secure, high-speed cloud servers to ensure lightning-fast loading everywhere. We provide you with a simple iframe embed code to place on your website, and we can also push the tour directly to your Google Business Profile for immediate local SEO impact.

Can we update menus or content later? +

Yes! The physical scan of your space remains permanent, but any digital information overlays—such as PDF menus, booking links, or video pop-ups—can be updated by our team at any time without needing to reshoot the venue.
